About frontMASS

We're community

Building a web community in Thessaloniki is of utmost importance for us. We believe that frontend techologies are a wonderful thing to work with and we believe tha many people in Thessaloniki have the same feeling. We want to bring all of them together.

We're the web

We want to cover a variety of web related topics. From Javascript to the backend or tooling to purely CSS architecture, we hope that we can cover a very diverse interest spectrum.

We're proffessional

All the speakers are well respected professionals working in either some of the hottest greek startups or large international companies. Even though this is a non-commercial event, the people talking have real world experience in demanding, high-paced environments.

Our Speakers

Thanos Papavasiliou

Thanos is a maker of digital experiences. Since 2006 he has worked with global brands, he enjoys selling things online, thinking big and and travel around the world. He’s currently based in a small village in Pieria called Svoronos, currently working in the most awesome startup in Greece as head of design.
Thanasis Polychronakis

Thanasis is a freelance web developer with a passion for doing Open Source, contributing to projects and helping the community. He is a seasoned web developer having worked for more startups than he can count. Thanasis’ frontend expertise lies with building large scale applications, complex build flows, automations and tooling.
Fotis Alexandrou

Fotis is a Web Developer that identifies himself as a full stack developer and tries to take full advantage of the tools offered by the community so he can be as productive as it gets. He thinks that development is more than a full time job hence, he codes all sorts of stuff, from web to shell and vice versa. He works at PeoplePerHour Athens HQ since November 2011.

Our Talks

  • Keynote

    11:00 - 11:50

    To Be Announced

  • How to make your designer love you more.

    12:00 - 12:50

    You are a frontend developer. You know all the dark secrets of Javascript, html5 & css3 is your everyday playground and building websites is your profession by day. Making designers love you is what will bring you closer to your dream job. In this talk you’ll understand why and how to make it happen.

  • CSS architecture for large scale applications

    13:00 - 13:50

    Clean, maintainable, reusable, performant. These are non functional requirements that the codebase of any application is expected to meet. And of course CSS is no exception. In this talk, we will see how we can architect our CSS when building a web application in order to meet those requirements. We will focus on CSS structure, naming conventions, best practices and tooling which large companies use to build their large scale applications.

  • Web developer workflow - Automation matters

    15:00 - 15:50

    How to streamline your web development workflow, from git to virtualization and deployment automation Fotis will dive into the cutting edge of today's development workflow. He will talk about Vagrant, what it can do for you, why you should know about it and how to leverage the most value out of it; Docker is the new kid on the block so naturally Fotis will acquant you with it, and finally a review of Puppet and how to use it for provisioning your deployment targets.

  • Frontend Tooling 101, from shell to browser

    16:00 - 16:50

    Thanasis will explain how you can do more in less time. Starting from the foundations of each developer, the Shell and going upwards to testing automation for your frontend assets. The journey of tooling will get us through leveraging watch tasks, livereload, modern build tools, precompilers, your editor and IDEs and dependency management for your frontend applications.
